Serimax SA Presentation

We are pleased to offer our company’s services for the various jobs we have carried out with experience and excellence throughout our history. Servimax SA has been providing various services in the oil, mining, welding, and other industries for over 30 years. These services include maintenance, operation, and gas treatment plants, as well as the supply of specialized workforce for tasks such as weed control and general maintenance of green areas in different zones of the oil fields operated by companies such as Tecpetrol SA, Pluspetrol SA, Shell CAPSS, Compañía General de Combustible CGC, Petroleo Sudamericanos, Necom SA UTE, among others.

Work Experience

Here is a summary of the work done during this time: • 1993-2003: Service, Operation, and Maintenance of the Oilfields provided by Tecpetrol S.A. • 1997-2002: PLUSPETROL S.A., specialized labor supply service for Gas Treatment Plant Maintenance and Operation in the Ramos Field Complex (Gas Treatment Plant Operators, Specialized Mechanical Officers, Specialized Electrical Officers, and Specialized Instrumentation Officers). • 1998-2003: PLUSPETROL S.A.: Service of two general task crews, welders, and pneumatic instrument technicians in the Ramos Field. • 1999-2001: SHELL CAPSA Company, in the Orán Department, Salta Province, Attention, Operation, and Maintenance of Surface Facilities in the Valle Morado Field. • 1999-2001: PLUSPETROL S.A. In the Palma Largo Field, within the Formosa Province, Attention, Operation, and Maintenance of Surface Facilities. • 2004-2007: Compañía General de Combustibles CGC, security, and equipment maintenance service. Later acquired by Gran Tierra Energy. • 2005-2008: Petróleos Sudamericanos S.A.- Necom S.A.- UTE. Attention, Operation, and Maintenance of Surface Facilities in the Caimancito Field, plus the provision of a crew for general tasks. • 2006-present: TECPETROL S.A. Integral Maintenance Service (Mechanical, Electro-Instrumentation) in the UTE AGUARAGÜE areas (Campo Duran, Chango Norte, Lomitas, Tranquitas, Río Pescado (Oran), and Aguaragüe). • 2007-present: TECPETROL S.A. With a team of 3 skilled crews for tasks such as clearing vegetation, masonry, assembly, and disassembly of pipelines, etc., in the UTE AGUARAGÜE areas (Campo Duran, Chango Norte, Lomitas, Tranquitas, Río Pescado (Oran), and Aguaragüe). • 2008-2009: Petróleos Sudamericanos S.A. Green Spaces Maintenance Service, well production testing, and Custody of company facilities in the city of San Ramón de la Nueva Oran, Salta. • 2009-present: Started predictive maintenance service for all valves in the UTE AGUARAGÜE Field for TECPETROL S.A. • 2009-present: Added a new service for TECPETROL, assembly and disassembly of mechanical pumping equipment (AIB), including predictive maintenance for that equipment. • 2011-2021: Provided welding services to TECPETROL with two qualified crews, including vehicle and tool provision. • 2012: Provided Electro-Instrumentation and Communication assembly services to TECPETROL, including vehicle and qualified personnel provision. • 2012: With a team of trained personnel in Communication and Electro-Instrumentation assembly, started a new service for TECPETROL S.A. in all production areas. • 2013: MAESTRANZA service for PLUSPETROL S.A. • 2014-2022: Civil construction work for community service promoted by TECPETROL in the rural area of Campo Duran and Algarrobal. • 2018: Civil construction work for community service in the town of Gral.
